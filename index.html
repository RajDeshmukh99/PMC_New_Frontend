  • arrow_drop_downOur Services
    Programs in the cultural centers are conducted in three sessions every day. Prestigious personalities including the Hon.President, Hon. Prime Minister, Hon.Union Ministers, Hon.Members of Parliament, Hon.Members of Legislative Assembly, Hon.Mayor, Hon. Commissioner, as well as senior officer bearers and leaders, grace some of these programs. On such occasions, it is our primary responsibility to take care of the entire arrangement including maintaining the cleanliness of the theatre, managing all the activities related to the stage, and taking care of everything right from receiving the V.I.P. guests till the time they leave for their next destination.

    Other important responsibilities of the Department include, managing 4-month slot reservations, organizing cultural festivals promoted by the Pune Municipal Corporation, imparting information under the Right to Information Act 2005, resolving problems that the managers, producers and artists may face by being available for them all through the day till 12 at night, following - up with pending issues, etc.

    Our staff works across three sessions of the day and night, i.e., from 8:00 am today to 8:00 tomorrow. Hence, our department offers 24 x 7 service. The overall work responsibility of the Cultural Centers Department is to provide services to the citizens for various forms of cultural, social, political, religious and educational programs; therefore, the cultural centres are included under the Essential Services of the government.
