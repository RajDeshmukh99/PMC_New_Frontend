ABOUT

The Bal Gandharva Ranga Mandir is owned by the Pune Municipal Corporation. Bal Gandharva Ranga Mandir is a theatre with an auditorium and an exhibition hall. It is one of the most sophisticated and well-equipped theatres of India. Late P.L. Deshpande, the renowned writer and humourist of Maharashtra, played an important role in the conception of this theatre.
The theatre is centrally-located in the Sambhaji Park area of Pune. It is looked upon as the main cultural destination of the city. It is available for cultural, educational, social and religious activities, as well as corporate and business seminars.

Location: ShivajiNagar, Near Rani Jansi Statue,JM Road, Pune.
Google Map: https://goo.gl/gcCWlS
Cornerstone Ceremony : Monday, 8th October 1962
Honoured By : Natsamrat Narayanrao Rajhans alias Bal Gandharva (the man himself)

Inaugurated By : Hon. Yashwantrao Chavan, Home Minister of India

Theatre Facilities
  • The auditorium has air-conditioning system installed.
  • Pay and Park facility is available.
  • Canteen and Cafeteria facility is available.
  • There are tenements available for the drama companies to keep their sets.
  • Display boxes are available for advertisements.
  • The Art Gallery is available for exhibition of handicrafts, paintings and works of upcoming artists.
  • There are various facilities offered to the artists including lodging and food.
  • V.I.P. Rooms are available for special guests.
